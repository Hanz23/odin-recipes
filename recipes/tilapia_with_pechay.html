Tilapia w/ Pechay Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pechay leaves
  • 1 pc tilapia
  • 1 mug water
  • 2-3 pcs tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 pc onion
  • Half-thumb sized ginger

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil.
  2. Sauteed the onions, garlic and ginger.
  3. Add the water as soup and the fish.
  4. Wait until the boiling or just before boiling.
  5. Add the pechay leaves. Wait for ~2-3 mins.
