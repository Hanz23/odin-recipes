Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken legs/thighs/breasts
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 1 pc onion
  • Black pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil.
  2. Sauteed the onions and garlic in cooking oil.
  3. Add 4-5 tablespoon of soy sauce and 2 tablespoon of vinegar.
  4. Add the chicken. Mix to make sure the entire part is covered in by the sauce.
  5. Wait to boil or until the meat is properly cooked/tender.
  6. Add black pepper.
