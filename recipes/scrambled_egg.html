Scrambled Egg Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 pcs tomato
  • 1 pc onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat cooking coil.
  2. Sauteed onion and garlic.
  3. Mix the eggs in a bowl properly.
  4. Add the eggs and sliced tomatoes.
  5. Mix until cooked.
  6. Add some salt and mix.
